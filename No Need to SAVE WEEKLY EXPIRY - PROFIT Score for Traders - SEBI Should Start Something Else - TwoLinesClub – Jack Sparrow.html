Definition

What is PROFIT?

PROFIT (Profitability Index for Traders) is a live, rolling score updated after every trading action—capturing win rate, expectancy, risk management, drawdowns, and consistency. Think CIBIL, but for trading competence.

Per-Instrument + Overall

Separate indices for Index Options, Futures, Stocks, Commodities—plus one consolidated Overall PROFIT that benchmarks total skill across instruments.

Access Linked to Proof

Segment & leverage access scale with verified PROFIT—not with titles or popularity. Results > Certificates

Why PROFIT Instead of Blanket Weekly-Expiry Bans?

Protect New Traders

Follow and learn only from high-PROFIT mentors; filter out noise and fake influence. Influencer status ties to audited results.

End One-Size Rules

Regulate access by ability, not by broad bans. High PROFIT unlocks advanced products; low scores guide to safer segments.

Reward Discipline

Consistency, controlled drawdowns, and risk hygiene are built into the score. Recklessness naturally ranks low.

Signals

What PROFIT Measures

  • Win rate & expectancy (R-multiple quality)
  • Max & average drawdown, recovery time
  • Volatility-adjusted returns (composite Sharpe-like blend)
  • Position sizing discipline & rule adherence
  • Consistency over rolling windows (30/90/180 days)
  • Slippage & execution hygiene (optional broker feed)

How PROFIT Solves Real Weekly-Expiry & Policy Concerns

Retail Losses

Low-score traders are auto-routed to safer segments until they improve. Education + guardrails, not exile.

Fake Influencers

Influence requires verifiable PROFIT. Public badges replace marketing hype and misinformation.

Manipulation

Score anomalies and cohort patterns make manipulation detectable without punishing everyone.

Over-Regulation

Shift from blanket bans to ability-based access. Regulators supervise the framework, not each trade.

Tax & Policy

Aggregated, anonymized PROFIT data quantifies retail value—strengthening the case for rational STT/GST.

Trust & Onboarding

Brokers, prop firms, and LPs can onboard by score tiers. Transparent, portable credibility for traders.

Implementation Blueprint

Data & Privacy

Opt-in broker APIs and audit uploads. Trader-controlled visibility (private, cohort, public). Anonymized for policy analytics.

Governance

Independent standards council (brokers, exchanges, auditors, trader reps). Annual methodology paper & third-party reviews.

Methodology

Composite score with caps/penalties for risk breaches, pyramiding abuse, and unmanaged drawdowns. Rolling windows to prevent gaming.

Access Tiers

Product & leverage ladders by score band. Mentorship/influencer rights only above threshold + proof-of-process.

FAQ: Weekly Expiry & PROFIT Score

Is PROFIT anti-regulation?

No. PROFIT complements regulation with a merit-based, data-driven layer that reduces systemic risk and protects beginners—without killing liquidity.

Does PROFIT support weeklies?

Yes. Weeklies add depth and affordable hedging. PROFIT preserves them while aligning access with proven discipline.

Who runs PROFIT?

An independent standards body with traders, brokers, exchanges, and auditors—published methodology and annual reviews.

Can this stop fake gurus?

Yes. Influence and mentorship privileges require a verifiable PROFIT threshold, wiping out non-performing hype.
