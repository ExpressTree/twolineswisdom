Chapter 2: The Naked Chart Revelation

My journey into this unconventional method began in December 2017. I made a simple yet profound decision: to strip my charts of all indicators. No RSI, no MACD, no moving averages, nothing. Just the naked price chart.

Why? Because I believed that the market is not random—it is driven by a force. And that force is none other than money.

Money, when inserted into the market, creates value. That value gets translated into a price. As traders, we assume and pay against this price, based on our perception of its elevation. This elevation—the difference between value and price—determines what happens next in the market.

Here’s the key insight: When price meets value, the elevation becomes zero. That point, where price and value align, is what I call the magical point. The longer the price waits at this point, the more powerful its next move becomes.

This is where the secret lies. Investing or trading from this zero-elevation point is the key to understanding market movements.

Chapter 3: The Formula of Price Action

Once I understood the concept of elevation, I derived a simple yet powerful formula:

New Value = Price + Projected Elevation

Where:

  • Price is the assumed entry point.
  • Projected Elevation is the expected movement based on action.

This formula is the essence of price action. Price alone is meaningless—it’s just a number on a screen. But action gives life to price. Every single rupee invested in the market has the power to move it.
